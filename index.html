How to play

Photograph every species in your field book. Fields, forests, towns, and water each have their own animals — and fish only show up while you're wading.

Computer

  • Mouse — look around
  • WASD — walk · Shift — run · C — sneak
  • Click — take a photo
  • Right-click — hold to zoom
  • Space — photo book · Esc — menu

Touch

  • Drag the view to look around
  • Joystick (bottom-left) — move; push to the edge to run
  • Shutter (bottom-right) — take a photo
  • Book (bottom-centre) · Cog (top-right) — save & load
  • Slider (right edge) — zoom

Scoring

  • Framing is everything: get the animal centered and filling the frame (walk closer or zoom in). Three stars are earned, not given.
  • Sizes: every animal rolls a size, teeny-tiny to beyond-real-life MASSIVE. Extremes are rare and worth much more. Tiny specimens score almost entirely on centering.
  • Combos: more animals in one shot = bonus points. The closest one gets the album page.
  • Birds in flight earn a 1.5× bonus — spook one, then catch it on the wing.
  • Sneak up on shy animals; cats and dogs will come to you. Only your best shot per species is kept.
