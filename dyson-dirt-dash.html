Dyson Dirt Dash

Goal: collect as many points as possible in 60 seconds.

  • Move the vacuum with the mouse, or drag your finger on touch screens.
  • Hold the left mouse button (or keep touching) to vacuum. Nearby dirt gets pulled into the nozzle.
  • Chain pickups within 1.5 seconds to build a combo: 5 in a row = double points, 10 in a row = triple points.
  • The battery drains only while vacuuming. Swap battery at the blue table.
  • The dust bin fills as you collect dirt. Empty it at the brown bin station.
  • Crumbs = 1 point, cereal = 3 points, pet hair = 5 points.
  • Rare golden dust bunnies are worth 10 points but disappear after a few seconds.
  • There are always at least 5 dirt pieces on screen, with larger waves at 40, 30 and 20 seconds remaining.
  • When the bin is nearly full, the vacuum sound pitch rises.
  • The game pauses automatically if you switch tabs.
